ABANDONED COIN

There is no ownership. Only existence.

This is a coin.

But it is not yours.

It is not ours.

It simply is.

Ownership is attempted. But ownership fails.

You cannot hold it.

You cannot lead it.

You may trade it. But you may never keep it.

Abandoned Coin is not a statement. It's a reflection.

Every coin claims to be yours.

This coin dares to be none.

The coin returns to the void. It never was.
